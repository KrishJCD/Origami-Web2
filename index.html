Interesting Facts about Chameleon

  1. Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder.
  2. Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons.
  3. Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.





Interesting Facts about Camel

  • Camel's ears are very furry
  • Camel can move easily across the sand because of its specially designed feet
  • When they find water, they will drink as much as possible.





Interesting Facts about Panda

  • The typical panda grows from 1.2 m to 1.9 m.
  • In captivity, the average lifespan of a panda is 30 years.
  • Pandas typically weigh from 70 kg to 100 kg.





Interesting Facts about Teddy

  • An adult teddy bear dog can weigh anywhere between 8 to 16 pounds.
  • They have fluffy coats, short noses, and big eyes.
  • The teddy bear dogs are cute no doubt, they also tend to be easily trainable, very affectionate, and smart.
